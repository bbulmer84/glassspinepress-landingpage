Privacy Policy

Glass Spine Press respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you provide when using this website. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights in relation to that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited personal information when you voluntarily provide it through this website.

This includes:

  • Your name (if provided)
  • Your email address
  • The contents of messages submitted through the contact form

We do not require account creation, and we do not collect sensitive personal information.

2. How Information Is Used

Any information you provide is used solely for the purpose for which it was submitted.

  • Responding to enquiries
  • Communicating about publications or requests you initiate
  • Improving the website experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Contact Forms and Third-Party Services

Contact form submissions are processed through third-party services to enable secure message delivery and spam protection. These services may temporarily process your information in order to deliver messages.

We only use reputable providers and do not store form submissions on the website itself.

4. Analytics and Cookies

This website may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with the site. These tools may collect anonymised information such as page visits and browser type.

No analytics data is used to personally identify visitors. You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the information submitted through this website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided.

To make a request, please use the contact page on this website.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us via the contact page.
